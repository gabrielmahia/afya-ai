# 🏥 AfyaAI — Kenya Health AI

> *Afya* (Kiswahili) — health.

Free AI health assistant for Kenya. NHIF contributions, hospital and clinic finder, maternal and child health guidance, and your health rights under the Constitution of Kenya 2010 — in English and Kiswahili.

## What it does

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 💰 **NHIF calculator** | Your monthly contribution, what it covers, and how to claim |
| 🏥 **Hospital finder** | Public and mission hospitals by county — with emergency contacts |
| 🤰 **Maternal health** | Antenatal care schedule, Linda Mama programme, birth registration |
| ⚖️ **Health rights** | Article 43 — your constitutional right to healthcare in EN/SW |
| 💊 **Symptom guide** | When to go to a dispensary vs hospital vs emergency |
| 📋 **Insurance literacy** | NHIF vs SHA, what's covered, how to appeal a rejection |

## Who this is for

- **Families in Kenya** who don't know what NHIF covers or how to claim
- **Kenyans in the diaspora** managing healthcare for relatives back home
- **CHPs and community health workers** who need a quick reference
- **NGOs and health facilities** who want a free patient information tool

## Quickstart

```bash
git clone https://github.com/gabrielmahia/afya-ai
cd afya-ai
pip install -r requirements.txt
streamlit run app.py
```

## Data sources

- NHIF Act (Cap 255) and SHA Act 2023
- Ministry of Health Kenya — facility registry
- Linda Mama Free Maternity Programme
- Constitution of Kenya 2010 (Article 43)
- Kenya Essential Package for Health (KEPH)

## Important disclaimer

AfyaAI provides **general health information**, not medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for diagnosis and treatment.
